Why Use Google Search Ads?

Google is the top used search engine currently on the market, in fact it receives over 63,000 searches per second. With this astronomical amount of daily searches, a company would be crazy to not want to reach these audiences that are already in the market for products.

However, ranking organically for a top spot in these searches would require extensive website work to build up an SEO strategy that would compete with the top players of every business vertical. And that’s where the benefits of Google Ads come in to play.

Google Ads allow smaller businesses to takeover the #1 spot from large corporations and receive the attention they deserve. Unseen auctions are occurring at lightning speed in the background to determine who shows up in these top spots when a search occurs. And that’s where we come in. We manage these unseen auctions through keyword research, bidding, ad copy testing, targeting so that you can reach the eyes of your target audience.
