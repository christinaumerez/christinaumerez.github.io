What is Digital Out of Home?

Digital outdoor advertising is a quickly advancing technology, infact is has increased 35% since 2010 and analysts predict it will overtake traditional print advertising spend by 2020. Apple, Netflix and Amazon are already embracing this technology to reach people when they are offline.

Display and video creatives can be used to appear to people during their daily commute, when they are out at restaurants, at airports, while grocery shopping and more. How is this different than traditional marketing? We are able to use expansive data to figure out exactly where your target market is and what time and only serve your ads to those you want. Digital out of home is also charged on an impression basis. There is little upfront cost similar to traditional marketing and is highly scalable with your business.

This marketing approach enables cross channel strategies to be implemented. Geo-location allows those exposed to your ad to be retargeted by pushing ads on their mobile device. This retargeting tactic increases the click through rate of mobile ads over 15% according to Outdoor Advertising Association of America.

Out of home also supplies metrics unsurpassed by print. It has the ability to leverage geo-location to provide measurement data such as where the ad is displayed, how many people have viewed your ad, where your target market
